About Company

MF Construction Lanka (Pvt) Ltd is a leading ISO 9001:2018 certified civil construction company in Sri Lanka, with over a decade of excellence in delivering sustainable and innovative infrastructure solutions.

We specialize in a broad spectrum of civil construction services and have established ourselves as a key player in the development of infrastructure that powers the future. Our expertise spans across transportation, road construction, bridges, water and wastewater treatment plants, urban development, and renewable energy projects. Guided by our core values of integrity, quality, safety, innovation, and sustainability, we continue to build infrastructure that shapes communities and supports economic growth.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka’s most trusted and innovative civil construction partner, delivering world-class infrastructure that enhances lives, empowers communities, and supports a sustainable future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build sustainable and innovative infrastructure that meets today’s needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own. We are committed to delivering projects that are environmentally friendly, socially responsible, and economically viable, while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and client satisfaction.

Roads & Bridges

Delivering solar power and other renewable energy projects that contribute to a greener Sri Lanka.

Sustainable Growth
