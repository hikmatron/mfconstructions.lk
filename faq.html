General Questions

Answers to common questions about our company, services, and how we work with clients across Sri Lanka.

What services does MF Construction Lanka provide?
We specialize in road construction & maintenance, bridge works, water & wastewater treatment facilities, urban development, and renewable energy (PV solar). We also provide project consulting, cost estimation, and project management.
Are you ISO certified?
Yes. MF Construction Lanka (Pvt) Ltd is ISO 9001:2018 certified, reflecting our commitment to robust quality management systems and continuous improvement.
What sectors do you serve?
We serve both public and private sectors—national and local government agencies, utilities, private developers, hospitality, industrial clients, and NGOs involved in infrastructure programs.

City Office (Colombo): 330/2C Temple Road, Kottuwala, Wellampitiya.

Services & Process

How we plan, design, and deliver infrastructure—from feasibility to handover and maintenance.

What is your typical project workflow?
We begin with feasibility studies and site investigations, proceed to detailed design and cost estimation, then move into construction, quality control, commissioning, and (where applicable) operations & maintenance support.
Do you handle design-and-build contracts?
Yes. We deliver EPC and design–build scopes across roads, bridges, water treatment, and solar PV, ensuring a single point of responsibility and streamlined delivery.
Can you support permits and approvals?
We coordinate with authorities for permits, utility interfaces, and environmental clearances, guiding clients through the regulatory process to avoid delays.
Do you provide maintenance after construction?
Yes. We offer preventive and corrective maintenance programs for roads, bridges, and treatment plants, including inspections, resurfacing, safety upgrades, and operator training.
What project information should clients share?
Site location and surveys, functional requirements, timelines, budget range, and any existing drawings or reports. This helps us respond quickly with accurate proposals.

Safety, Quality & Compliance

Our commitment to safety, quality management, and sustainable construction.

What safety measures do you follow on-site?
We implement strict HSE plans, toolbox meetings, PPE compliance, hazard identification, and emergency response procedures. Safety inductions and regular audits are mandatory for all staff and subcontractors.
How do you ensure quality?
Our ISO 9001:2018 QMS governs materials approvals, method statements, ITPs (Inspection & Test Plans), laboratory testing, and final acceptance criteria—ensuring performance and durability.
Do you follow environmental best practices?
Yes. We adopt erosion control, waste minimization, dust and noise management, and responsible material sourcing. Our renewable energy work further supports national sustainability goals.
Are your teams trained and certified?
Our engineers, supervisors, and technicians undergo regular technical and HSE training. We also onboard specialist partners for advanced scopes when required.
Do you provide documentation at handover?
Yes. We deliver as-built drawings, test reports, O&M manuals, warranty documents, and training for client operators where applicable (e.g., treatment plants and PV systems).

Bidding, Contracts & Careers

Information on tenders, commercial terms, and joining our team.

How do you participate in tenders?
We participate in competitive tenders and invited bids. Share tender documents or RFPs with us via mfclanka@gmail.com and our team will review eligibility and submission timelines.
What commercial terms do you work with?
We work under various models—lump sum, remeasurement, EPC/design–build, and maintenance contracts—aligned with client requirements and project risk profiles.
Do you collaborate with subcontractors and suppliers?
Yes. Prequalified subcontractors and suppliers are welcome to register with us. Send your company profile and scope to mfclanka@gmail.com.
Are there internship or graduate opportunities?
We regularly onboard interns (Civil Engineering, Quantity Surveying) and consider graduate trainees. Watch our Careers page or email your CV and cover letter to mfclanka@gmail.com.
How quickly can you mobilize for projects?
Mobilization timelines depend on project size, approvals, and site readiness. For standard scopes, we typically mobilize within a few weeks of contract award.
