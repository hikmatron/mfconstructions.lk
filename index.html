About Company

MF Construction Lanka (Pvt) Ltd is a leading ISO 9001:2018 certified civil construction company in Sri Lanka. With over a decade of excellence, we specialize in delivering sustainable and innovative infrastructure that powers the future.

Our expertise spans across road construction, bridges, water and wastewater treatment plants, urban development, and renewable energy projects. We are committed to integrity, quality, safety, and sustainability in every project we undertake. With a dedicated team of engineers and professionals, we strive to exceed client expectations by completing projects on time, within budget, and above industry standards.
